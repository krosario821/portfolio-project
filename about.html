About Me

Hi! I'm Kailey Rosario, a passionate computer science student who loves blending creativity with technology. As a first-generation Hispanic American raised by immigrant parents, I carry a deep pride in my heritage and the resilience it's instilled in me.

I'm currently working toward my Associate’s in Computer Science while building a portfolio that showcases my interests in digital content, web design, and programming. My goal is to use technology as a tool to inspire and uplift others—especially women and minorities in tech.

Since 2021, I’ve also been training in Taekwondo, which has taught me discipline, perseverance, and the importance of mental strength. It’s more than a sport to me—it's part of my journey toward personal growth.

When I’m not coding or kicking, you’ll likely find me spending time with kids and animals, dancing around the house, journaling, or listening to music. I value honesty, joy, and creativity in everything I do.

Long-term, I hope to blend my love for technology with my traditional values. My dream is to build meaningful software, raise a big, loving family, and live a life rooted in connection, purpose, and faith.
